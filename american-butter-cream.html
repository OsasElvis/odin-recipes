American Buttercream

Home

Descripton

American buttercream is a classic frosting used for cakes and cupcakes. It's known for its smooth, creamy texture and rich buttery flavor.

Ingredients

  • Butter - 400g or 500g
  • Icing sugar - 1kg
  • Flavour (optional)
  • Milk (optional) - 3tbsp

Directions

  1. Add the butter to a mixing boel and mix to soften.
  2. Sieve icing sugar.
  3. Add icing sugar into butter and mix properly.
  4. Add flavour.
