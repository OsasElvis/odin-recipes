Plain Cake

Home

Description

A plain cake is a simple cake made with basic ingredients like flour, sugar, eggs, butter, milk, and baking powder. It has a slightly sweet flavor and a light and fluffy texture. Because it's unflavored and undecorated, a plain cake is a versatile base that can be dressed up with frosting, fruit, chocolate sauce, whipped cream, or any other toppings you like.

Ingredients

  • Flour - 500g
  • Butter - 400g
  • Sugar - 400g
  • Golden syrup (optional) - 2tbsp
  • Milk - 250ml
  • Buttermilk - 1 cup milk and 1tbsp vinegar
  • Eggs - 10
  • Nutmeg (optional)
  • Pinch of salt
  • Baking powder - 2tbsp
  • Flavour
  • Baking soda - a pinch
  • Alcohol (preservative) - 1/2 cup

Directions

  1. Cream butter and sugar using a mixer or spatula till it becomes fluffy or double in size.
  2. In a seperate bowl, mix/seive together all dry ingredients.
  3. Whisk eggs in a seperate bowl till soft peak.
  4. Pre-heat oven to 1800C and grease pan.
  5. add eggs, then dry ingredients to the creamed butter (in batches or in bits).
  6. Add milk and flavour, mix on medium speed.
  7. Pour batter in greased pan and bake.
