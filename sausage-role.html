Sausage Roll

Home

Descripton

This is a recipe for the classic Nigerian sausage roll.

Ingredients for Dough

  • Flour - 500g
  • Butter -200g
  • Nutmeg - 1/8 tsp
  • Baking powder - 1/4 tsp or 1/2 tsp
  • Salt - 1/8 tsp
  • Egg yolk - 1 or 2
  • Milk (optional)
  • Water

Directions for making the dough

  1. In a bowl add flour, salt, baking powder and mix them together.
  2. Add butter and mix until it becomes bread crumbs.
  3. Add eggs and water and mix gently until all is combined.
  4. Wrap dough and set aside in a cool place for 15 - 30 mins.

Ingredients for filling

  • sausage
  • Garlic
  • Onions (1 medium size)
  • Ground pepper
  • Seasoning and salt to taste
  • Flour (as thickener)
  • Pink food colour

Directions for preparing the filling

  1. Put all ingredients in a blender and add a little water.
  2. When blended, add a little flour and pink food colour and mix in the blender.
  3. When done pour the blended mixture into a bowl and add little flour until it is thick.
  4. Roll ot the dough in batches and add filling to it.
